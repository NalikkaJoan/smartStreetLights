Description

The LDR senses whether its day time or night considering the rays from the sunlight. It reports to the arduino which decides which action to take that is to say if it is day time the lights are off ad if it is night time it informs the IR sensors. It also counts how many vehicles and pedestrians passed by the street on a particular day. The IR sensors on the other hand detects motion and if there is none the lights are dim or off and if there is any motion then the light switch themselves on.
